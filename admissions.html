Admissions

Join our vibrant learning community

Admission Process

1. Application Form

Fill out the online application form or download and submit the physical form at the school.

2. Entrance Assessment

Students undergo a simple assessment appropriate to their age and class level

3. Interview

Parent and child interview with the School Director

4. Offer Letter

Successful applicants receive an official admission offer.

Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate or age declaration
  • Previous school report(if applicable)
  • 2 passport photographs
  • Immunization record
  • Transfer certificate(for students above Primary 1)
